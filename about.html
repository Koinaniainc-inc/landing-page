About Us

We’re reimagining how enterprises manage data by solving the chaos of schema evolution with automation and AI.

Our Mission

To empower developers, engineers, and data teams with intuitive and intelligent tools for schema versioning, integration, and observability — reducing downtime and increasing productivity.

Our Origin Story

Koinonia.ai was born from a real pain experienced in growing dev teams — managing constantly evolving schemas, dealing with failed migrations, and lacking unified visibility across environments. Our founders, with backgrounds in data engineering and DevOps, set out to fix this problem from the ground up.

What We Believe In

  • Automation over repetition
  • Open standards, not vendor lock-in
  • Visibility and control across every schema change
  • Empowering teams, not replacing them
